Our History & Mission

Founded in Hamilton, Ontario, Rexford Peters Chambers has built a reputation for legal excellence through decades of dedicated service to our community. Our firm was established on the principles of integrity, professionalism, and unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Our mission is to provide strategic legal counsel that protects our clients' interests while upholding the highest standards of the legal profession. We believe in personalized attention and tailored solutions for every case we undertake.
